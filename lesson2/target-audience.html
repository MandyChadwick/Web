Description

Who:

My target-audience is those living in Southeast Idaho who need to know the weather in order to do outdoor activities. This group of people are very active and would like to know which activity to do on a given day.

Persona 1

Nathan Fackrell is a single 18 year old male

About Nathan

Nathan has an associates of arts which he earned the last two years of high school. He works at a local school

Why Nathan is using this site

Nathan is an avid biker and would like to know about storms that may develop during the day.

Persona 2

Carol Jean is a 70 year old Female

About Carol

Carol is an avid gardner. She is older and retired. She is frugal and a good manager of her resources.

Why Carol is using this site:

Carol would like to know if she needs to water her garden this week. She is looking mostly at the rain forecast.


Scenarios

  1. A user would like to know if the weather is suitable for biking.
  2. A user would like to know if it will rain this week.
  3. A user would like to go for a drive but would like to known the wind speed first.
  4. A user needs to know if their garden will freeze.
  5. A user is curious if it is warmer in a neighboring town.
