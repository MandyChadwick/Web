Description

Who:

My target-audience is those living in Southeast Idaho who need to know the weather in order to do outdoor activities. This group of people are very active and would like to know which activity to do on a given day.

Personas 1

Jessica Hughes is a single 24 year old black female.

Occupation and Education:

Full-time student at Baylor University, has a Bachelor's in child education and is currently enrolled in a master's program. He has a part-time job at Wal_Mart, her current income is less than $20,000 a year

Goals and Motivation for using the site

Uses this sight for vacation planning. It is used to check weather forecast, current events, and relevant news for the area.

Social

Jessica loves the outdoors, her favorite place to visit is Fish Haven especially Bear Lake. She loves water sports, boating, water skiing, jet skis, and wake boarding. She and her family vacation there every year.

Technology

Jessica is very comfortable with technology. She uses smartphone, tablet, and laptop regularly. She has social media accounts and networking accounts.
