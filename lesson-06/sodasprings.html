We Had Fun at Geyser Days !

What fun we had at Geyser Days! Hopefully, you were able to join us. There was a marathon salmon feast in the park and, of course, the 9:00 am viewing of the Geyser erupting. This event brought happiness to all who attended. There was family fun provided by the local American Legion and some family history booths provided by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Sue Dickson, a local artist, found the event quite exciting. She said, “I have come to this event each year for a decade now. I find something exciting each time. My favorite part is the Salmon dinner. It is great to know that the proceeds go to the local Boys and Girls Club!” This event was for all ages. A 6-year-old elementary student named Jessica Bethel told us “I loved seeing the runners. My mom and older brother ran the marathon last year and I hope to be able to run it when I am 13.” The Geyser is something to celebrate! Old Faithful, look out because our Soda Spring Geyser is one of the best.
