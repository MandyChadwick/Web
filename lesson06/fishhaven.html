Fishing Never Gets Old

For Paul Fox, fishing never gets old! He has been fishing for fun and sport for over 35 years. "The fish in this area don't know how much I know about them." He told Weather Brother, "I have been fishing this creek long enough to have caught their ancestors." He is the 4th generation of fishermen in this area. We asked him what his favorite fish story is, and this is what he said, "When I was just five, my dad took me to the lake. I had never fished before. He had always gone by himself." He told me I was now old enough to learn how to fish quietly. Sure enough, we caught several trout that day. I will never forget what they tasted like roasted over a fire fresh out of the creek. All it took was a little salt and pepper, and I knew I was going to be a fisherman like my dad!" The local Hatchery supports fishing in Fish Haven. We are glad they keep the fish coming for locals and visitors. Keep up the excellent work.
